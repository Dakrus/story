Adam’s Story

Adam Forgione tells stories for Sheryl Crow, Goodyear, HBO, Warner Bros., Motorola, TGI Friday’s and many others. Adam is the creative director and founder of Pennylane Visuals – his studio shoots national television commercials, feature-length documentaries, music videos for Grammy Award winners, Fortune 500 corporate pieces, and million-dollar weddings has allowed him to master his storytelling skills working for some of the most elite clientele on the planet.

Adam will teach you to master emotion and tell amazing stories, so that you deliver home runs for your clients every time.

Adam prides himself on sharing his knowledge and experience with filmmakers around the world. Adam’s style of explaining even the most complex subjects allows filmmakers of all skill levels to understand and apply his techniques.

Before he was a filmmaker, Adam was an accomplished musician and music producer, playing keyboards for Ritchie Blackmore’s (Deep Purple, Rainbow) world tours and producing and engineering many pop/rock albums for local New York bands. It’s no wonder why he’s been coined “The Audio Guru” of filmmaking.
